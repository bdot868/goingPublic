Who We Are

We define, engage, and influence audiences to generate measurable business results for our clients.

Going Public PR is comprised of an innovative team of communications strategists who focus on key elements of Entertainment, Brands, Lifestyle, Media and Digital Strategies. Our communications strategists have helped shape and define many brands, talent, TV shows and companies. Each client is served by a highly customized plan to bring forth the best level of expertise to drive measurable results. We ensure that the most creative and strategic approach is taken when addressing the client’s goals as they relate to communication, branding and media.

At GPPR, we strongly believe in taking a proactive approach and creatively seeking out the right press opportunities for our clients. We are not a company that believes "all press is good press." Additionally, we seek out impressionable opportunities that will forward a client’s vision and goals and deliver maximum results. Our agency is uniquely positioned within the entertainment, tech, business and nonprofit communities to be a portal for you to engage with key editorial, online, and broadcast press. Our established relationships also extend beyond the media—to influencers and tastemakers that will create buzz in the lifestyle, business entertainment landscapes.

WHAT WE DO

Corporate Communications

We help clients convey their mission, vision and values to its stakeholders and the public. Corporate communications involves managing and orchestrating all internal and external communications aimed at creating a favorable footprint among stakeholders on which the company depends. We generate messaging on behalf of a corporate organization or entity issued to its audiences, such as employees, media, partners, target audiences and the public.

Crisis Management

Your brand is only as good as its reputation. It is crucial that you curate and protect that reputation. It’s how your customers see you, talk about you, and think of you. We can help you maintain, enhance and protect your reputation.

In the era of social media, your online reputation is also critical to your company’s success. We can help foster compelling messaging and social media strategies, as well as key positioning and positive search results on the web for your company.

Digital & Social Media Engagement

We are committed to helping our clients tackle key challenges and opportunities in strategic, innovative, efficient, and effective ways. We further enhance their success in the Digital Era through an alliance that provides thought leadership, guidance, research, consulting, as well as a professional community that fosters the sharing of information and best practices.

In essence, we develop a strategic, integrated approach to leveraging social and digital technologies for organizations and their staffs.

Growth & Niche Markets

We connect brands to multicultural and millennial communities. We also connect faith based organizations and social impact businesses to broader audiences. We are very successful in creating custom communities and networks. When these audiences feel connected to each other in addition to the brand, they are more likely to develop stronger relationships with the brand itself.

We know how they think, where they are, and how they want to be spoken to. We provide clients with our proprietary insights and analytics for these communities. Our methodologies in mining these networks make for prosperous connections.

Lifestyle & Brand Marketing

We can assist you in forming your unique brand identity. Everything a company does should ladder up to its identity. This service includes brand messaging as well as how and where you speak to your target consumers/audiences.

For brand management, we can advise on analysis and planning on how your brand is positioned in the market, which target audience your brand is targeted at, and maintaining a desired reputation of your brand. Developing a good relationship with target audiences is essential for brand management. The intangible elements are the experience that the consumer takes away from the brand, and also the relationship that they have with it.

Media & Public Relations

We work with media with the objective of informing the public of an organization's mission, policies and practices in a favorable, consistent and credible manner. The goal of media relations is to maximize positive coverage in the mass media without paying for it directly.

We create, enhance, develop and manage relationships with journalists to procure and secure key placement in the media to take our clients public.

Meet Our Team

Tanya Diggs

Tanya M. Diggs is a marketing consultant with over 20 years of experience in advertising, brand marketing and strategy. She has worked for top multicultural advertising agencies as an Account Director at Walton Isaacson and as the Vice President of Engagement Marketing and PR for Burrell Communications Group for the Lexus and Toyota brands, respectively. Ms. Diggs has also held senior level marketing positions with Sony Electronics, Honeywell Aerospace, AT&T, Gateway Computers and other internet companies and is YouTube Certified.

As a marketing consultant, her primary role has been to develop business plans and strategies focusing on creating new revenue streams by generating excitement and brand affinity with targeted general market and multicultural consumers. Within advertising and consumer markets, her experience spans across multiple industries in Strategic and Integrated Marketing, Alliances and Partnership Marketing, as well as Business Development. Ms. Diggs received both her Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and an M.B.A. from the University of Southern California Marshall School of Business. She is currently a board member of the Mobile Film Classroom, which provides digital and media education and instruction for underserved youth.

Karen Lewis

For over twenty years, Karen Lewis has served as Founder and President of Going Public PR, a boutique PR communications resource for talent, corporate and media clients. She has built an innovative team comprised of communications strategists, who provide strategic insights, tactics and activations across varied categories. Ms. Lewis specializes and has an expertise in consumer engagement, media relations, digital/social media engagement, corporate communications, faith-based outreach, multicultural & millennial marketing, reputation management, and social responsibility.

Currently headquartered in Los Angeles, her agency’s services scale to both national as well as regional activation (sample markets include Atlanta, Chicago, Dallas, Houston, Miami, New York, Los Angeles, Philadelphia, San Francisco, and Washington DC). Ms. Lewis is a native of Los Angeles and a graduate of the University of California at Berkeley. She currently serves as Advisor to the Watts Community Healing Garden, the only cleantech garden of its kind, as well as sits on the LA Council of the National Women’s History Museum.
